Israel Cancer Research Fund

The Israel Cancer Research Fund (ICRF) is a nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting innovative cancer research conducted by Israeli scientists. Since its founding in 1975, ICRF has awarded over $77 million in research grants to Israeli scientists, resulting in significant breakthroughs in cancer research and treatment methods.

Alpha Epsilon Pi has proudly supported ICRF's mission to find treatments and cures for cancer through our philanthropic efforts.
